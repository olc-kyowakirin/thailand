    Overview

    Kyowa Kirin (Thailand) Co., Ltd. is which is one of subsidiary companies of Kyowa Kirin Co., Ltd. under Kirin Holdings Company, Limited in Japan.

    Kyowa Kirin is an R&D-based life sciences company with special strengths in biotechnology. The Kyowa Kirin Group companies strive to contribute to the health and well-being of people around the world by creating new value through the pursuit of advances in life sciences and technologies.

    Kyowa Kirin’s core competency is its expertise in leading-edge biotechnology and antibodies. Using these strengths, we continually work to discover innovative new medicines in our chosen therapeutic areas of oncology, nephrology and immunology/allergy, and to bring these products to the global markets. As a Japan-based specialty pharmaceutical company with global aspirations, we aim to make a real difference to healthcare and to peoples’ lives around the world.
